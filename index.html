I am an undergraduate student studying mathematics at the University of Washington. I am interested in analysis, partial differential equations, and probability, and their applications to optimal transport, machine learning, and biological systems. Currently I am working in collaboration with the Allen Institute as a Visiting Scientist to research and develop ML and optimal transport frameworks for single cell differentiation. I am also drafting my thesis titled "Optimal Transport: Dynamic and Unbalanced Formulations" under Professor Dan Mikulincer. In addition to my academic work, I enjoy spending time in the outdoors as well as working on personal projects related to mathematics, machine learning, data science, and more.
